About the WebApp

Welcome to our Currency Converter tool! This handy tool allows you to quickly and easily convert between different currencies, helping you to stay updated with the latest exchange rates and make informed financial decisions. Whether you're traveling abroad, making international purchases, or simply curious about currency conversions, we've got you covered.

How to Use:

1.Enter Amount: Enter the amount you wish to convert in the "Amount" field. The equivalent amount in the target currency will be calculated automatically based on the latest exchange rate.

2. Select Currencies: Begin by choosing the currency you want to give amount

3. Result: All the results from different country will display.
